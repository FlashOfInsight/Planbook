About the Planbook

By keeping all academic information organized in one place, we strive to make it easier for Dalton students to keep on top of the sometimes overbearing workflow.

Many people helped to make the Online Planbook a reality.

Davis Haupt '17

Wrote the original code for the Online Planbook over four years.

Zachary Buttenweiser '15

Assisted overall in all of the engineering challenges we faced, and contributed his code for logging in with Dalton IDs.

Alex Kaplan '17

Worked with faculty to make the Planbook a sustainable project, from 2012 through 2015.

Andrew Milich '15

Being the High School President the year the Planbook first went into effect, Andrew helped the project to truly become part of the Dalton's student experience.

Nick Lechich '06

Assistant Director of the High School for Student Life at the inception of the Planbook, Mr. Lechich was the bridge between the admininistration and the students during the project.
