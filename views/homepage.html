This is a template for a simple marketing or informational website. It includes a large callout called a jumbotron and three supporting pieces of content. Use it as a starting point to create something more unique.

Stay on top of it all.

The online Planbook is designed to do what you already use your planbook for every day, but better. Write down your assignments on your laptop, and they are instantly synced to your phone, iPad, or any other device, so there's no fear of losing your planbook. Color codes help you keep track of what is important.

Set Reminders

With the online Planbook, you can set reminders for important assignments. Want to remember to study before a test? You set the interval, and the frequency of the reminder.
